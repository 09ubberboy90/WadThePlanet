About us

WadThePlanet is a project made for educational purposes as part of the WAD2 coursework. The objective of our application is to provide space enthusiasts a place to express their imagination about what distant planets might look like. It is easy to get started even for users who are not proficient in 3D modelling or digital art.
Any technical information regarding this project can be found at its GitHub repository.
